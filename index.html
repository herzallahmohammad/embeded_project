Concept

In this tutorial, we’ll learn how to use the AS608 Fingerprint sensor with Arduino. For that, I used the Arduino UNO board, LCD screen, LED bulb, and resistor, but you can use any other Arduino board. This tutorial is important because it can help you make biometric attendance and registration systems, Home security systems, door lock systems, safety boxes, and more, depending on your needs.

Motivation:

The motivation behind developing a Smart Attendance System lies in addressing several challenges associated with traditional attendance tracking methods, such as paper-based registers or manual entry systems. Here are some key motivations:

Accuracy and Reliability:

Traditional attendance systems are prone to errors, either due to human error in recording attendance or through proxy attendance. Smart Attendance Systems utilize technologies like biometrics (fingerprint, facial recognition), RFID (Radio Frequency Identification), or GPS (Global Positioning System) to ensure accurate and reliable attendance tracking.

Efficiency and Time-saving:

Automating the attendance process saves time for both students and teachers. It eliminates the need for manual data entry and reduces administrative overhead, allowing educators to focus more on teaching and students to engage in learning activities promptly.
